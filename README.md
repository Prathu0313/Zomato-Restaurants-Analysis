# 🍽️ Zomato Restaurants Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides an in-depth analysis of Zomato restaurant data across multiple countries, cities, cuisines, ratings, and customer services. The project helps identify restaurant distribution, popular cuisines, ratings trends, online delivery adoption, and table booking availability.

The dashboard enables users to explore restaurant performance and customer preferences through interactive visualizations and filters.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze restaurant distribution across countries and cities.
- Identify the most popular cuisines.
- Evaluate restaurant ratings and customer votes.
- Compare online delivery and table booking availability.
- Discover top restaurant chains based on cuisine count and ratings.

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 🌎 Country Distribution
- India has the highest number of restaurants in the dataset.
- Restaurants are spread across 15 countries including the USA, UK, UAE, Australia, Singapore, South Africa, and others.

### 🏙️ City Analysis
Top cities by restaurant count:
1. New Delhi – 5,473
2. Gurgaon – 1,118
3. Noida – 1,080
4. Faridabad – 251

### 🍜 Popular Cuisines
Top cuisines by restaurant count:
- North Indian – 3,960
- Chinese – 2,735
- Fast Food – 1,986
- Mughlai – 995
- Italian – 764

### ⭐ Ratings Analysis
- Average restaurant rating: **2.89**
- Majority of restaurants fall within the **3–4 rating range**.

### 🚚 Online Delivery
- Available: 2.45K Restaurants
- Not Available: 7.1K Restaurants

### 🪑 Table Booking
- Available: 1.16K Restaurants
- Not Available: 8.39K Restaurants
